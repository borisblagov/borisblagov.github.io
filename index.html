Welcome

I am an economist and a professional forecaster for RWI – Leibniz Institute of Economic Research in Essen, Germany at the division Macroeconomics and Public Finance.

I am part of the junior faculty of the Ruhr Graduate School of Economics and teach at the Jacobs University in Bremen.

On this page you will find my research, forecasts, replication codes, and teaching materials.
